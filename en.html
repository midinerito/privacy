1 Who we are

Mi Dinerito in one sentence

Mi Dinerito is an Android mobile application that helps grown-ups teach finance to their children aged 5 to 12 via an educational five-jar system (Spend, Rent, Save, Give, Invest) and family-based approvals.

The app is exclusively owned and operated by Angel Miguel Friero Calvo, resident in Spain. This policy describes the technical decisions implemented in the product.

2 Philosophy

Privacy is part of the product

These decisions are implemented in the code:

  • We do NOT use analytics services. No Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Crashlytics, no Google Analytics.
  • We have NO advertising within the application. Zero Ads SDKs. Zero mediation.
  • We do NOT share or sell data to third parties for commercial purposes.
  • We do NOT track individual usage or behavior within the app.
  • We do NOT use the data to train AI models.
  • We do NOT request location (neither precise nor approximate).
  • We do NOT transmit AAID, Android ID, Build Serial, BSSID, MAC, SSID, IMEI, or IMSI.
  • We do NOT request the device phone number.

Photos the grown-up attaches to a kid's wish stay exclusively on the device where they were taken. They are never uploaded to a server, never visible on other devices. If the kid takes the photo on the grown-up's tablet, it's only visible from that tablet; the grown-up, from a different device, will see the emoji the kid picked.

Minors do NOT need to create a Google account or provide an email. They sign in via a single-use code generated by one of their parents and linked to a Firebase Anonymous Authentication session. Binding requires two-step adult approval.

3 Data we collect

What goes in, what doesn't, and why

3.1 From grown-ups (parents / legal guardians)

  • Email associated with the Google account. Required for authentication and to identify members of the same family. Stored in Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud, Europe region, eur3).
  • Family nickname and configuration: family name, currency, language, savings jar nickname.

3.2 From children

  • Nickname chosen by the grown-ups. We do not request real name.
  • Age range (5–7 / 8–10 / 11–12), not date of birth.
  • In-app usage data: balances of the five jars, completed tasks, proposed wishes, unlocked achievements, movement history. Linked to an anonymous identifier (Firebase Anonymous Authentication UID) that does not allow identifying a real minor.
  • Wish photo (optional): when a grown-up attaches a photo to a kid's wish, that photo stays exclusively on the device of the grown-up who took it. Only the file path is stored in the database; the photo content never leaves that device.

3.3 Technical data (not controlled by us)

Google Play Console automatically collects aggregated data about Android versions, device models, and app crashes as part of its service. This data is aggregated and not individually identifiable by us.

This page is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub logs visitor IP addresses for security purposes — this is NOT controlled by the author. See GitHub's policy: docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies.

6 GDPR legal basis

Why we can process the data

According to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and the Spanish LOPDGDD:

  • Grown-ups: consent on Google sign-in and accepting this policy (Article 6.1.a GDPR) and service contract execution (Article 6.1.b GDPR).
  • Minors: parental consent exercised by the grown-up creating the family and binding the kid via single-use code (Article 8 GDPR). The grown-up grants consent on behalf of the minor.
7 Where data is stored

Storage and international transfers

  • Cloud Firestore on Google Cloud Platform, region eur3 (Europe).
  • Firebase Authentication for session tokens.
  • Local device storage (Hive) for settings, parent-mode PIN, and cache.

Google Cloud applies industry-standard technical and organizational security measures (encryption at rest and in transit, ISO 27001, SOC 2 / 3).

We do NOT transfer data internationally outside the European Economic Area.

8 Retention

How long we keep the data

  • While your family is active: data kept for the app to work.
  • After pressing "Delete family" in the grown-up panel: permanent deletion within 24 hours (includes data of the grown-up and all kids in the family).
  • After uninstalling without pressing "Delete family": data remains in Cloud Firestore in case you reinstall. Request deletion at angel.friero@proton.me.
9 Your rights

How to exercise your GDPR rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): delete all your family's data.
  • Portability: receive your data in structured format (JSON).
  • Opposition / withdraw consent at any time.
  • Restriction of processing under certain circumstances.
  • Lodge a complaint with the AEPD — Spanish Data Protection Agency — if you believe your rights have not been respected: aepd.es.

To exercise any of these rights, write to angel.friero@proton.me. We respond within 30 calendar days.

10 Children and minors

Special safeguards for minors

Mi Dinerito is designed specifically for family use with minors. We implement the following technical safeguards:

  • Minors sign in via a code generated by a grown-up in the family, not via self-registration.
  • Kid access requires two-step approval: code + grown-up approval of the binding from their panel.
  • We do not request personally identifiable information from minors: no email, no real name, no date of birth, no phone number.
  • Photos stay exclusively on the device of the grown-up who took them.
  • The grown-up can delete the kid's account or the entire family at any time.
  • Every virtual money movement by the kid passes through parental approval. The kid never executes transactions directly.
  • We never show advertising under any circumstance.
  • We comply with the Google Play Families Policy and European legislation applicable to minors (Article 8 GDPR).

If you believe a minor has provided data without parental consent, contact angel.friero@proton.me immediately and we will proceed with deletion.

11 Cookies and similar technologies

What is stored on your device

Mi Dinerito is a native mobile application and does not use HTTP cookies. We do use:

  • Local device storage (Hive): stores parent-mode PIN, language preference, and confirmed family IDs. This data is NOT sent to any server or third party.
  • Firebase Authentication session tokens: to keep your session active across app launches.

This web page does depend on GitHub Pages infrastructure, which logs IP addresses for security (see §3.3). The page itself does not load external fonts, does not use Google Fonts, does not include third-party scripts, and does not set cookies.

12 Security

How we protect data

  • All communications with Cloud Firestore are encrypted in transit via TLS 1.2+.
  • Encryption at rest on Google Cloud servers (ISO 27001 / SOC 2 standard).
  • The application binary is distributed signed with the author's own RSA-2048 certificate (Google Play Signing).
  • Cloud Firestore security rules restrict each family's data access exclusively to its approved members. Defense in depth client + server.
  • Parent-mode PIN is stored only on the device (encrypted Hive), never on remote servers.
  • Kid device binding requires two-step approval with single-use codes and 10-minute TTL. A leaked code does not grant access on its own.
